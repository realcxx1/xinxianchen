I am a PhD student in Financial Engineering at [Xiamen University].

My research interests include financial econometrics, multivariate volatility
and dependence modeling, heavy-tailed and skewed distributions.

## Research Interests

- Financial Econometrics
- Multivariate Volatility and Dependence
- Heavy-Tailed and Skewed Distributions
- Asset Return Predictability


## Working Papers

### Split-Session Cluster GARCH for Heavy-Tailed Multivariate Dependence

This paper develops a dynamic multivariate dependence model that distinguishes
between overnight and intraday trading sessions and accommodates heterogeneous
tail behavior.

### Convolution Skew-t Distributions

This project develops a standardized multivariate convolution skew-t
distribution with block-specific skewness and tail parameters.

### Macroeconomic Uncertainty and Stock Return Predictability

This paper studies whether supervised aggregation of macroeconomic uncertainty
improves stock return prediction.
